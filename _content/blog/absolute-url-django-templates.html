Read https://docs.djangoproject.com/en/dev/ref/templates/api/#django-core-context-processors-request. Then to your template add:

{% verbatim %} {{ request.build_absolute_uri|slice:":-1" }}{% url 'some-view' %} {% endverbatim %}
